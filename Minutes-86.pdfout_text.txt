included in the adopted budget, are authorized by Education Code

Section 42602. These resolutions provide the means by which
boards

after they have adopted a final budget. When such added income 1s
available and a majority of the board is in agreement as to its use,
the

a routine action. It increases the budgeted appropriations in the
amount of the added income and authorizes the expenditure of that
