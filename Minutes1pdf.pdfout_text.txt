OPEN SESSION (2:30 p.m., Auditorium)

CLOSED SESSION (Room 2249)

Closed session to conference with the District's labor negotiator,
pursuant to Government Code Section 54957.6, regarding collective
bargaining with the San Diego Education Association.

OPEN SESSION

PLEDGE OF ALLEGIANCE

SUPERINTENDENT'S INFORMATION REPORT

Superintendent Bersin announced tentative agreement had been
reached to reform (extend) the current collective negotiations
contract between the District and the San Diego Education
Association to a new three year term, beginning July 1, 2000 and
continuing through June 30, 2003. The Board 1s expected to take
action to approve the new contract at their regular meeting on
September 26, 2000.
